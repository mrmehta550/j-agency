Security is integrated into our CI/CD pipelines. We follow OWASP guidelines, implement strict IAM roles, use automated vulnerability scanning, and conduct regular penetration testing.
Will I own the source code?
Absolutely. Once the project is completed and fully paid, all Intellectual Property (IP) and source code are fully transferred to your organization.
Do you provide post-launch maintenance?
Yes, we offer ongoing SLA-based support, DevOps monitoring, and maintenance packages to ensure your application stays updated and runs smoothly.
